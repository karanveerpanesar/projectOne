Let's tune your baby up first!

Tune your guitar on the standard tuning. For reference, from top to bottom, the strings go E, A, D, G, B, E

Let's play SNAKE with your Guitar!

The first 4 strings of your guitar (E,A,D,G) can be used to control the motion of the snake.

  • E is LEFT
  • A is UP
  • D is RIGHT
  • G is DOWN
